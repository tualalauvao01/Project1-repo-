Manhole Covers

Manholes are (public) networks owned by the Wellington City Council and located in both public land, such as roadways, and private land.

Manhole Cover One

This first CCTV camera is a camera outside a hostel located on the Terrace in Central Wellington.

Manhole Cover Two

A second CCTV camera which is not so hidden as it has a sign to warn people. This is inside the same hostel as the first camera and is different in its design.

Manhole Cover Three

This tiny camera located in the same hostel as the first and second cameras. Much more smaller and it's placement is in right in front of the main office.

Manhole Cover Four

A camera I saw as I got off an elevator down Lambton Quay. This was right outside a dairy in a small enclosed area so of course there has to be a camera in this area.
