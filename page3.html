Electricity in Wellington

A business called 'Wellington Electricity' is an electricity distributor. They manage poles, wires and equipment. Wellington Electricity safely deliever electicity to about 166,000 homes and businesses in Wellington, Upper hutt and Porirua regions of New Zealand. Major infrastructure providers such as WEllington Airport use a significant amount of electricity. Wellington Airport are major customers of Wellington Electricity. Electricity is important because we need it for light, for water, to cook and also to access the internet.
